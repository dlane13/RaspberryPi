Remote Conveyor Belt Operation

This conveyor belt prototype models the idea of a conveyor belt mounted on the build plate of the 3d printer. As soon as a build is complete, it will be moved off of the plate so that the next project in the queue can begin immediately. The button seen on this page allows you to control this conveyor belt from anywhere, with ease.
